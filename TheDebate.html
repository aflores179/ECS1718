Why are scientists considering this option?

Colonized planets would serve the same purpose as space stations initially in that they would provide insight into the possibility of having a human civilization on another planet if a catastrophe so extreme that humans would have to leave Earth were to occur. Earth is running out of resources and although humans aren not short of land, we are coming close to using all of our arable land. With a new planet comes a new start and scientists around the world are working towards giving mankind that opportunity.
